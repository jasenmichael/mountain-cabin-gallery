Mountain Cabin

I was working on another project in Livingston, Montana, and would often go to a coffee shop down the street. After a couple of months I began to talk to the Coffee shop owner about a cabin he dreamed of having built on his property nearby. I immediately was like "I can put something together for you."

The cabin site was in the mountains about seven miles from Livingston, but the last three miles had no road, or electricity (so a generator was needed). As I got closer to finishing the current project the cabin plans were coming together; budget, time-frame and material lists being manifested. I lined up another contractor to do the grading on-site, and build a road. The cabin was to be built in a 2 week time-frame squeezed between when I finished the current project and my next obligations. Right on schedule as I finished my current project, the grading, road, and first load of materials were paid for, and scheduled for delivery.
